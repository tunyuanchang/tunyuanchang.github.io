About Me

My name is Tun-Yuan Chang (張惇媛). I am currently a master’s student at Institute of Information Systems and Applications, National Tsing Hua University (NTHU). I received a B.S. degree from Department of Computer Science with a minor in Quantitative Finance at National Tsing Hua University (Sep. 2020 - Jun. 2024). My research interests include Multimodal Large Language Models, Machine Learning, Smart Cities, Digital Twins, and related fields.
